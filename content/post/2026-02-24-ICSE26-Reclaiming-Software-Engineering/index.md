We are pleased to announce that our position paper "Reclaiming Software Engineering as the Enabling Technology for the Digital Age" has been published in the ICSE FoSE track (ICSE'26).

The paper argues for reinvesting in software engineering as a strategic discipline essential to the sustainability, dependability, and sovereignty of digital technologies. The full publication details, authorship and abstract are available on the publication page: [Publication details](/reference/ICSE26-FoSE/).

You can also read the PDF on HAL: [hal.science/hal-05468508](https://hal.science/hal-05468508).

Congratulations to all co-authors!
